About Me

I am a board-certified physician specializing in Infectious Diseases and Internal Medicine, with clinical and research expertise in Clostridioides difficile infection, the microbiome, and infection in older adults. I serve patients in Newport Beach, California, at Southern California ID Associates, and previously held an Assistant Professor position at the University of Virginia School of Medicine, where I led clinical and research efforts in complicated C. difficile infection.
